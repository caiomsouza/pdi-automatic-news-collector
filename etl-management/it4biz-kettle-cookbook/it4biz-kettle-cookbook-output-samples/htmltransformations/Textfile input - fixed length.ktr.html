Load the example fixed length textfile.
The used test data are from an opinion poll in 1994 where
451 persons at the University of Mainz, Germany where
interviewed by Jens Bleuel about the knowing and usage of 
Internet, CompuServe, BTX (a german online provider in 
former times) and JoguBits (an IT-Journal at Mainz University).
I was happy as I found this data treasure on a dusty CD.
Have fun to dig out your own findings about the Internet-usage
in ancient times ;-)
